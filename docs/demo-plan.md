# NetGuardian Demo Plan

## Five-minute Video Flow

1. Explain the business problem: SOC teams drown in logs and need safe AI assistance.
2. Show the architecture: Enterprise Digital Twin, Event Bus, Incident Engine, ADK-style agents, human approval, MCP execution.
3. Reset the demo.
4. Run the Alice malware telemetry sequence.
5. Show risk score and incident creation.
6. Show AI investigation evidence and business reasoning.
7. Approve isolation.
8. Execute isolation through MCP.
9. Show verification: C2 stopped, endpoint isolated, `FILE-01` and the Payroll Database safe.
10. Mention evaluation, Docker Compose deployability, and Kaggle track.
